About company

Minery.io was founded in 2018 and opened for operations four months later. We built our first site in Siberian city of Bratsk to provide our clients with hydroelectricity at very competitive rates. We partnered with Bratsk Energy Generating Company to establish reliable and scalable energy supply.

We consider the trust of our customers to be the most precious commodity and we work hard to earn it. In 2018 our company was certified by IDACB (International Decentralized Association of Cryptocurrency and Blockchain) and successfully fulfilled the anti-scam verification.

Our company has built a network of business connections with major manufacturers of cryptocurrency mining hardware. From experience we learned about risks and pitfalls of every model on the market. We created our own service center where our trained experts repair and service ASICs.
